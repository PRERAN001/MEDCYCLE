Our 5-Step Process

Medicycle follows a rigorous, environmentally responsible process to handle medical waste and redistribute usable medications:

Collection

Through drop-off locations, mobile units, and scheduled pickups, we gather unused medications from households, pharmacies, and healthcare facilities.

Over 5,000 collection points established nationwide.

Sorting & Assessment

Our pharmacists carefully evaluate all medications, separating usable ones from those requiring disposal, checking expiration dates and packaging integrity.

100% of medications undergo rigorous quality checks.

Redistribution

Safe, unexpired medications are repackaged with proper labeling and distributed to free clinics and underserved communities through our partner network.

Partnered with 50 organizations to ensure efficient distribution.

Eco-Disposal

Expired medications are processed using environmentally responsible methods, including high-temperature incineration at licensed facilities.

100% safe disposal rate, minimizing environmental impact.

Reporting

We maintain transparent records and provide detailed reports to regulatory bodies while using data analytics to improve our processes.

Annual impact reports published for transparency and accountability.

About Us

Sustainable Medicine Management Starts Here

Medicycle is built for individuals, communities, and healthcare providers who want to revolutionize medicine management and promote sustainability. Whether you're a household tracking medications, a pharmacy managing inventory, or an NGO helping those in need, we provide the tools to reduce waste, recycle responsibly, and ensure access to essential medicines.

Our platform offers real-time medicine tracking, expiry alerts, and a community-sharing network—all in one intuitive app. With features like emergency medicine locators, a marketplace for required medicines, and eco-friendly disposal guidance, we help you minimize environmental harm while fostering a circular economy for medicines.

From connecting neighbors to share unused medicines to educating users on green healthcare practices, Medicycle adapts to your needs and scales with your impact. Join us in creating a sustainable healthcare ecosystem—one medicine at a time.
